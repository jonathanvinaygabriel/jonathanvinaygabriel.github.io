Smart Retailer - A Customer Segmentation and Recommendation Product

With the help of this project, I built a tool that creates a machine learning model which uses Market Basket Analysia and RFM Analysi to find correlations between items in customers' carts and segment them based on their Recency, Frequency and Monetary Value. Also, using Item Based Collaborative Filter it finds the customer shopping patterns to recommend relevant products based on their history of purchases and viewing.
